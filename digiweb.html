About Us

“DIGITAL COMPETENCY IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR OF ACCOUNTANCY PROFESSION: UNDERSTANDING THE IMPACT ON PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE IN LUCENA CITY” concludes that digital competencies significantly enhance accountants' productivity, accuracy, and decision-making by enabling efficient task completion, effective collaboration, and real-time financial analysis. While digital safety skills have a weaker impact, continuous upskilling remains crucial for maximizing efficiency in an increasingly digital profession. Thus, this platform provides valuable resources to help accountants improve their digital skills through studies, instructional videos, and research-based materials.
